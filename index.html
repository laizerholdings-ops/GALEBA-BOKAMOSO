Established Excellence

Innovative Civil Engineering Experts

GALEBA BOKAMOSO (PTY) LTD is a premier 100% black-owned construction firm based in Welkom, Free State. We specialize in delivering high-performance infrastructure solutions, from initial road foundations to professional paving for the industrial, commercial, and residential sectors. Our goal is to blend technical precision with aesthetic excellence, ensuring every project stands the test of time.

Our Mission

To build sustainable infrastructure through integrity and superior craftsmanship across the Free State and beyond.

Our Vision

To be South Africa's most trusted partner in large-scale paving and development, known for reliability and quality.

Our Values

We prioritize safety, 100% customer satisfaction, and building durable foundations in every square meter.
